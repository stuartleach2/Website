About Me

Hello! I'm Stuart, a devoted husband and proud father of two wonderful neurodivergent children, living in Harrogate, North Yorkshire. My passions include photography, travel, architecture, and reading.

Professionally, I am fascinated how technology can make the world a better and more inclusive place for all of us, particularly in healthcare and education.

My qualifications include an MSc in IT and Digital Futures with a DevOps specialization, and certifications as a Scrum Master and Product Owner. I am skilled in Lean and Agile principles, SAP solutions, process improvement, change management, project and program management.

To learn more about my professional life, please visit my LinkedIn profile.
