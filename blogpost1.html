Agia Effimia

June 10, 2024 by Stuart Leach

How this site came to be built using only an iPhone at 6am while sat on a sun lounger in the hills overlooking Agia Effimia in Kefalonia, from code written by GPT-4o.

Photography: Capturing Moments

Photography is one of my greatest passions. It allows me to capture and preserve moments in time, to tell stories through images, and to explore the world around me in a unique way. Whether I’m traveling to new places, exploring architectural wonders, or simply enjoying a day out with my family, my camera is always by my side.

Technology: Shaping the Future

With over 20 years in IT leadership, I’ve had the opportunity to witness and contribute to incredible advancements in technology. I’m particularly interested in how technology can improve our lives and solve some of the world’s most pressing problems. In my current role, I focus on delivering IT solutions that support and enhance business strategies, with a keen eye on innovation and digital transformation.

Travel: Exploring New Horizons

Traveling is a passion that goes hand in hand with my love for photography. There’s nothing quite like exploring new places, experiencing different cultures, and capturing the beauty of the world through my lens. Each journey offers a fresh perspective and new inspiration for my work and personal life.

I hope you enjoyed this first post. Stay tuned for more updates, stories, and insights from my adventures in photography, technology, and beyond.

Thank you for reading!

– Stuart Leach
